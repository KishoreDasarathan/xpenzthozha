Frequently Asked Questions
Forgot your password?
Email us at xpenzthozha@gmail.com with proof of account ownership. We can manually delete your old account so you can re-register with the same username.
How do I add expense categories?
Go to Dashboard → Add Category. You can create custom categories like Food, Travel, Bills, etc. Default investment categories (Gold, SIP, Silver, PPF) are created automatically on signup.
Is my financial data safe?
Yes. All passwords are hashed using Werkzeug security. Your expense and investment data is isolated per user — no one else can access it. We don’t sell or share data.
Can I export my data?
Currently export is not available, but we’re building CSV/Excel export for both expenses and investments. Expected in the next update.
Why can’t I see my old account?
Each username/password combo creates a separate account. Make sure you’re using the exact username you registered with. Usernames are case-sensitive.
Does XpenzThozha work on mobile?
Yes! The site is fully responsive. Add it to your home screen for an app-like experience. Native mobile apps are on our roadmap.
